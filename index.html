About

We built what we wished existed

Driftline was founded after seeing firsthand how digital platforms struggle to detect fraud before damage is done. Most tools act after the fact. We act during the conversation.

🎯 Our Mission

To make digital platforms safe for everyone. Trust is the foundation of every platform — protecting it should be simple, affordable, and automatic.

🔒 Privacy by Design

We never read message content. We analyze only metadata — timestamps, reply speeds, conversation counts. Compliant with GDPR and CCPA by default.

⚡ Built for Scale

Our infrastructure processes millions of events per day with sub-50ms response times. Whether you have 1,000 users or 10 million, Driftline scales with you.

🤝 Partner First

We work closely with our platform partners to tune risk models for their specific context. Every platform is different — we account for that.
