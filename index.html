About Driftline
We built what we wished existed

Driftline was founded after seeing firsthand how peer-to-peer marketplaces struggle to detect fraud before damage is done. Most tools act after the fact. We act during the conversation.

🎯 Our Mission
To make peer-to-peer marketplaces safe for everyone — buyers, sellers, and the platforms that connect them. We believe trust is the foundation of every marketplace, and protecting that trust should be simple.
🔒 Privacy by Design
We never read message content. We analyze only metadata — timestamps, reply speeds, conversation counts. This makes Driftline compliant by default with GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations worldwide.
⚡ Built for Scale
Our infrastructure processes millions of events per day with sub-50ms response times. Whether you have 1,000 users or 10 million, Driftline scales with you without configuration changes.
🤝 Partner First
We work closely with our marketplace partners to tune risk models for their specific context. What's suspicious on a rental platform differs from a service marketplace — we account for that.
